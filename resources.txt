### Resources used
-Web Font Loader used to load google fonts asynchronously. This reduces
the amount of time the DOM is blocked from rendering.
  -https://github.com/typekit/webfontloader

-Using transform: translate instead of left to animate the moving pizzas.
This reduces the amount work the browser does to animate the pizzas.
  -http://www.html5rocks.com/en/tutorials/speed/high-performance-animations/

-Optimizing loops in JS
    -http://conceptf1.blogspot.com/2014/01/javascript-best-practices-loop-optimization.html
